White Rabbit is the name of the daemon that actually runs and scores all the checks that are loaded for this competition. In order to know how to score the checks and what information is needed, it implements three of the four basic check types: Service, Inject, and Attacker. More information can be found in the Introduction along with the reasoning behind each type of check.
